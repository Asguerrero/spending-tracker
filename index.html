Frequently Asked Questions

How does Spending Tracker work?

You send a quick text message whenever you spend. That's it. If you want to include a note to remember what the spending was for, you can.

Is Spending Tracker reliable?

Yes. People have been using the Spending Tracker to manage their out-of-pocket spending for more than a year. It works like a charm.

Can I stay in sync with my partner?

Yes. Spending Tracker works just like a text message thread. Everyone sees what everyone else enters.

Does Spending Tracker spy on me?

Absolutely not. Spending Tracker only sees what you text to it. It does not connect to any of your accounts. We do not share or sell your data, ever.

How does it know what I spend?

When you send a text message to Spending Tracker, it looks to see if the message starts with a number. Spending Tracker reads that number and updates your balance.

Does Spending Tracker fit my needs?

Spending Tracker is a family-run business. We are in this together with you to ensure our product fits your needs now and in the future.

Can I reset my balance?

Yes. When you type a + at the start of your text message, the Spending Tracker adds the amount to your balance. This way you can track refunds, correct mistakes, and reset your balance any time you want.

Do I need a budget and categories?

Not at all. With Spending Tracker, you just enter your spending as it happens. Spending Tracker shows you in real time how you're doing.
